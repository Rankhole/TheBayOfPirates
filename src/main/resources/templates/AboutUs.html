Project goals:

  1. Enable (legal) torrent sharing/down- and uploading
  2. Build a solid foundation for a torrent-caring community
  3. Rating system for torrents

Torrents themselves are not illegal, they are just a way of sharing files with other people, regardless of whether they're legal or illegal. Since torrents utilize peer-to-peer networking, it's possible to "host" files without actually having a server. This project is obviously not discussing the legality or ethicality of piracy. Piracy can be done with or without torrents! This is not the topic of this project.
